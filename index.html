Our specialties

Specialized care for children to speak, connect, and grow.

Articulation

Helping individuals improve the clarity of their speech by addressing sound errors and pronunciation difficulties.

Language Development

Enhancing language comprehension and expression for children and adults facing challenges in vocabulary, sentence structure, or understanding.

Social Communication Skills

Developing conversational and nonverbal communication skills for individuals with autism, social anxiety, or pragmatic language impairments.

Voice Therapy

Addressing vocal strain, hoarseness, or other voice disorders to restore healthy and effective communication.

Fluency

Helping individuals manage stuttering or other fluency challenges to improve speech flow and confidence.

Early Intervention

Developing skills that form the foundation for reading and writing, such as recognizing sounds in words, rhyming, syllable segmentation, and blending sounds.
